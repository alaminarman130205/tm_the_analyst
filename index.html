Intro

As a dedicated and detail-oriented Data Analyst, I bring a robust set of skills in SQL, Tableau, Power BI, Excel, R, and Python to the table. With a comprehensive background in data analysis, I have honed my ability to interpret and visualize complex data sets, providing actionable insights that drive strategic decision-making. My expertise in SQL allows me to efficiently query and manage databases, ensuring data integrity and accessibility..

Proficient in Tableau and Power BI, I create compelling and interactive dashboards that enable stakeholders to understand key metrics and trends at a glance. My advanced Excel skills further support data manipulation and analysis, allowing for the creation of sophisticated models and reports. With a strong foundation in R and Python, I am adept at performing statistical analysis and building predictive models, enhancing the ability to forecast and optimize business outcomes.

My analytical approach is grounded in a deep understanding of data structures and algorithms, complemented by practical experience in machine learning and data visualization. Whether working independently or as part of a team, I am committed to leveraging data to uncover insights, solve problems, and contribute to the success of my organization.
